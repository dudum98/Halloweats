BOOZY BUTTERBEER! (Alcoholic Option)

Harry Potter fans, who are over 21 years old, are you not entertained?!

This boozy twist on the classic butterbeer is not to be missed! No doubt it will be a hit at your next Harry Potter themed party!


Ingredients

FOR THE WHIPPED TOPPING
2 c. heavy cream
2 tbsp. packed brown sugar
1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
2 tbsp. melted butter
Gold sanding sugar, for garnish
Butterscotch syrup, for garnish


FOR THE PUNCH
6 (12-oz.) cans cream soda
3 (12-oz.) cans seltzer
3 1/2 c. vanilla vodka

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine cream, brown sugar and vanilla. Using a hand mixer, whip until soft peaks form. Fold in melted butter and set aside.
  2. In a punch bowl, combine cream soda, seltzer, and vodka. Top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of gold sanding sugar.
  3. Drizzle butterscotch syrup on rim of mugs. Serve punch and top with additional sanding sugar and butterscotch syrup, if desired.

Dietary Info

Contains dairy

Contains alcohol

Serving Size

9 servings

Prep Time:

10 mins


Mix Time:

10 mins


Total Time:

20 mins


Quick Instructions:

  1. Make whipped topping using ingredients listed
  2. Make punch using ingredients listed
  3. Decorate glass with syrup and add additional toppings to cream as desired

BLUEBERRY RICKETY EYEBALL PUNCH! (Non-Alcoholic Option)

Who said you can't have fun without alcohol?

This delicious, fruity, and spooky concoction is perfect for the non-alcoholic party goers!


Ingredients

12 to 24 fresh blueberries
12 canned lychees in syrup, drained, plus 5 to 6 tablespoons syrup from the can
3 cups blueberry juice cocktail
1/3 cup freshly squeezed lime juice (about 4 limes)
2 cups sparkling water
Ice

Instructions

  1. Stick 1 to 2 blueberries, depending on their size, into the cavity of each lychee.
  2. Place on a foil-lined baking sheet and freeze, about 1 hour.
  3. Stir together 5 tablespoons of the lychee syrup, the blueberry juice and lime juice in a bowl or pitcher until combined.
  4. Stir in the sparkling water and add the lychee eyeballs.
  5. Adjust syrup according to taste
  6. Serve in tall glasses over ice, spooning the lychee eyeballs into the glasses as you pour.

Dietary Info

Vegan

Gluten free

Serving Size

6 to 8 servings

Prep Time:

10 mins


Mix Time:

10 mins


Total Time:

20 mins


Quick Instructions:

  1. Make eyeballs using lychee and blueberries
  2. Make punch using ingredients listed
  3. Decorate as desired
